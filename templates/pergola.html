Under the Pergola
Smashes, Spins, and Garden Wins

Welcome to the back garden battleground where legends are made and laughter reigns supreme. For years, table tennis was a holiday treat—casual rallies on borrowed tables, cheeky tuition from schoolmates and work colleagues, and the occasional surprise spin that left us grinning. Hayley’s grandma and grandad were the original champions, hosting garden matches that echoed with joy and the clatter of paddles. The kids played under their watchful eyes, learning the rhythm of the game and the rhythm of family.
And now, at last, we have our very own table. A place not just for play, but for memory-making. The rallies are longer, the spins sharper, and the laughter louder. This is where the Hedaux league begins.
