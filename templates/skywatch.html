Skywatch
A Window to the Ever-Changing Canvas Above
Skywatch Gallery Live Feed Logbook
Every image captured here is a quiet moment of wonder—whether it’s a drifting cloud, a passing plane, or the fleeting silhouette of a bird in flight. This project blends automation and curiosity: photos are collected from my Raspberry Pi setup and sorted with the help of AI into categories that reflect the skies’ shifting stories. Skywatch is a space for reflection, observation, and the joy of noticing what often goes unseen.
